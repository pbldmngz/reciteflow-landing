Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

ReciteFlow ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application ReciteFlow (the "App").

Our Privacy Commitment

ReciteFlow is designed with privacy in mind. We believe you should be able to memorize your lines without being tracked, profiled, or monetized. Here's our commitment:

  • No accounts required - You don't need to create an account or sign in to use ReciteFlow.
  • No personal data collection - We don't collect, store, or transmit any personal information.
  • No tracking or analytics - We don't use any analytics services, tracking pixels, or user behavior monitoring.
  • No ads - We don't display advertisements and don't share data with ad networks.
  • Offline-first - The app works entirely offline. Your data never leaves your device.

Data Storage

All data you create in ReciteFlow is stored locally on your device only. This includes:

  • Your memorization sets (lyrics, scripts, speeches)
  • Study progress and spaced repetition data
  • Folders and organization
  • App preferences (language, session length, settings)

This data is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties because we don't have servers that collect user data.

In-App Purchases

ReciteFlow offers an optional one-time Pro purchase through Google Play. The purchase transaction is handled entirely by Google Play, and we do not receive or store any payment information. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for information about how Google handles purchase data.

Export/Import Feature

You can export your sets as .reciteflow files to share or backup. These files are created locally on your device. When you share them, you control where they go. We never see or access these files.

Children's Privacy

ReciteFlow does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since we don't collect data, there is no special data handling for children.

Third-Party Services

ReciteFlow uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Billing - For processing Pro purchases (optional). See Google's Privacy Policy.

We do not use any analytics, crash reporting, or advertising services.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
