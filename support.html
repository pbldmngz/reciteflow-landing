Frequently Asked Questions

Is ReciteFlow really free?

Yes! ReciteFlow is free with up to 3 memorization sets, full SSRS learning, performance deadlines, and export/import. Pro ($4.99 one-time) unlocks unlimited sets, hint modes, folders, and light theme.

Does it work offline?

Absolutely. ReciteFlow is designed to work completely offline. All your data stays on your device. No internet needed, ever.

What is SSRS?

Sequential Spaced Repetition System. Unlike traditional flashcards that show content randomly, SSRS teaches lines in order (the way they appear in your text) while using spaced repetition to schedule reviews. Weak lines come back sooner, strong lines get spaced out.

How do performance deadlines work?

Set a date for your show, audition, or presentation. The algorithm automatically compresses review intervals as your deadline approaches, pushing harder on lines you haven't mastered yet. Everything converges to readiness on performance day.

Can I share sets with castmates?

Yes! Export any set as a .reciteflow file (with or without study progress) and share it via any method: messaging, email, cloud storage, etc. The recipient imports it directly into their app.

How do I restore my Pro purchase?

Go to Settings in the app and tap "Restore Purchases." Make sure you're signed into the same Google account you used for the original purchase.

Is there an iOS version?

Not yet, but it's on the roadmap! Follow for updates on when the iOS version will be available.
