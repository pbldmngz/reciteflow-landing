Built for memorization, not flashcards

ReciteFlow isn't a generic flashcard app with a theater skin. Every feature is designed around how performers actually learn text.

Core

Sequential Spaced Repetition (SSRS)

Lines are introduced in order, not randomly. You learn line 1, then line 2, then line 3 — the way text actually flows. Spaced repetition schedules reviews so you see weak lines more often and strong lines less. It's the best of both worlds: sequential learning with scientific spacing.

Smart

Performance Deadlines

Set a performance date and the algorithm compresses review intervals automatically. The closer your show, the more aggressively it schedules weak lines — so everything converges to mastery right when you need it.

Smart Section Detection

Paste any text and ReciteFlow detects verses, choruses, bridges, scenes, and paragraphs automatically.

Hint Modes

First letter hints, fill-the-gap, or full blind. Gradually remove cues as you get stronger. Pro feature.

Folders

Organize sets by show, class, project, or however you think. Drag and drop to rearrange. Pro feature.

Share & Export

Export sets as .reciteflow files with or without study progress. Share with castmates or back up your work.

English & Spanish

Full bilingual interface. The app detects your device language or you can switch manually at any time.

Offline & Private

Everything stays on your device. No accounts, no tracking, no internet needed. Just you and your text.
